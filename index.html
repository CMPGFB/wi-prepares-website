Essential Supplies

üíß Water Supply

  • 1 gallon per person per day

  • Water filters & purification tablets
  • Storage containers & testing strips

üçö Shelf-Stable Staples (2-4 weeks supply)

"Rice, Beans, Canned Meat... Pancake Mix, Honey/Jam/Syrup... & a Can Opener. Store what you'll actually eat."
