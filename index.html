Essential Supplies

💧 Water

  • 1 gallon per person daily
  • 2-week supply recommended
  • Filters & purification tablets

🍚 Food

  • Rice, pasta, canned goods
  • Protein sources (beans, peanut butter)
  • Ready-to-eat snacks

Pro Tip: Store items you like eating—and rotate them.

🏥 Medical Kit

  • First-aid supplies
  • 7-day prescription backup
  • Sanitation & masks

Safeguarding Vital Info

📂 Documents

  • IDs & insurance papers
  • Medical records
  • Emergency contacts

💾 Backups

  • Electronic copies
  • Portable drives
  • Cloud storage

🔗 Alerts

  • Sign up for local warnings
  • Download alert apps

Quarterly Maintenance

🔄 Supplies & Storage
  • ✔️ Rotate food (FIFO method)
  • ✔️ Check water & medication expiration dates
  • ✔️ Recharge power banks & solar gear
  • ✔️ Restock first-aid & batteries
🧾 Documents & Records
  • ✔️ Update ID, benefit docs, insurance info
  • ✔️ Print new emergency contact sheets
  • ✔️ Backup to encrypted USB & cloud
🏠 Home Infrastructure
  • ✔️ Test smoke/CO alarms
  • ✔️ Clean gutters, inspect roof & basement
  • ✔️ Check HVAC or heat source
🎒 Go Bags & Bug-Out Kits
  • ✔️ Rotate seasonal clothes
  • ✔️ Refresh meds & snacks
  • ✔️ Check flashlights & phone chargers
📡 Info & Alerts
  • ✔️ Test NOAA radios and emergency apps
  • ✔️ Re-check alert systems for local area
  • ✔️ Bookmark key agencies (DHS, FEMA)
🛐 Spiritual & Community
  • ✔️ Refresh scripture cards or devotional printouts
  • ✔️ Call elderly neighbors or single parents
  • ✔️ Volunteer for local food/supply drives
💵 Financial Readiness
  • ✔️ Check emergency savings & cash on hand
  • ✔️ Consider alternative assets
  • ✔️ Adjust budget to reflect seasonal costs
