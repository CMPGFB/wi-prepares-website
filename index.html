Essential Supplies

💧 Water

  • 1 gallon per person daily
  • 2-week supply recommended
  • Filters & purification tablets

🧂 Food

  • Rice, pasta, canned goods
  • Protein sources (beans, peanut butter)
  • Ready-to-eat snacks

Pro Tip:

Store items you actually like eating—and rotate them.

🏥 Medical Kit

  • First-aid supplies
  • 7-day prescription backup
  • Sanitation & masks

Safeguarding Vital Info

📂 Documents

  • IDs & insurance papers
  • Medical records
  • Emergency contacts

🗀 Backups

  • Electronic copies
  • Portable drives
  • Cloud storage

🔗 Alerts

  • Sign up for local warnings
  • Download alert apps
